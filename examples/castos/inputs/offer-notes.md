# Offer Notes

Castos helps people host, distribute, analyze, and manage podcasts from one platform.

Key product areas:

- public podcast hosting
- private podcasting
- analytics
- distribution
- WordPress publishing with Seriously Simple Podcasting

## Who It Is For

- brands running podcasts as a marketing channel
- agencies producing podcasts for clients
- course and community businesses using private audio
- WordPress-heavy creators and businesses
- established podcasters who want more control than beginner tools provide

## Why People Buy

- They want reliable podcast hosting.
- They want private podcasting without duct-taping tools together.
- They want WordPress publishing control.
- They want analytics and distribution in one place.
- They have outgrown a basic hosting setup.
