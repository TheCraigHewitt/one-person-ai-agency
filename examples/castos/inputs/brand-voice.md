# Brand Voice

## Voice

- practical
- clear
- calm
- helpful
- operator-minded

## Phrases To Use

- podcast hosting that gives you control
- publish from WordPress
- private podcasting for teams, courses, and communities
- host, distribute, and grow your podcast

## Phrases To Avoid

- crush it
- go viral
- 10x your podcast
- secret podcast hack
- passive income

## Tone Notes

Talk to serious publishers. Avoid beginner hype.
