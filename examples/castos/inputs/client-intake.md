# Client Intake

Client: Castos
Website: https://castos.com
Industry: Podcast hosting / podcasting software
Primary offer: Podcast hosting, private podcasting, analytics, WordPress publishing, and podcast distribution tools.
Price / pricing model: Subscription SaaS.
Target buyer: Brands, creators, agencies, educators, and businesses that publish podcasts or private audio.
Sales motion: Self-serve SaaS with content-led acquisition.
Current marketing channels: SEO, content, podcasting, partner/referral, email, and experiments with paid acquisition.
Meta ads objective: Generate qualified trials and landing page conversions from people with a clear podcasting use case.
Primary conversion: Start trial / sign up.
Known constraints: Avoid generic "start a podcast" messaging. Focus on serious publishers with a business reason to host and distribute audio.

## Notes

- Castos has strong WordPress relevance through Seriously Simple Podcasting.
- Private podcasting is a meaningful differentiator for internal training, courses, and membership content.
- Meta ads should avoid sounding like beginner creator bait.
