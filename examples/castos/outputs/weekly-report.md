# Weekly Report - Castos

Sample data for demo only.

## Executive Summary

The private podcasting course/community angle produced the lowest sample CPL at $12.50. The WordPress control angle produced a solid CPL at $17.86 and may be more strategically differentiated. The agency workflow and internal training angles need more testing before deciding.

## What Changed

Four sample ad angles were compared:

- WordPress Control
- Client Podcast Workflow
- Private Audio Course
- Internal Training Audio

Total sample spend: $900
Total sample leads: 48
Blended sample CPL: $18.75

## Winners

### Private Audio Course

Spend: $200
Leads: 16
CPL: $12.50

This is the strongest sample performer. The likely reason is that the use case is specific and easy to understand.

### WordPress Control

Spend: $250
Leads: 14
CPL: $17.86

This is worth continuing because it is differentiated and likely attracts a more qualified buyer.

## Losers

### Internal Training Audio

Spend: $200
Leads: 7
CPL: $28.57

This may be too abstract for a cold Meta audience without stronger creative or proof.

### Client Podcast Workflow

Spend: $250
Leads: 11
CPL: $22.73

This could still work, but needs agency-specific proof or a sharper pain point.

## Hypotheses

- Specific use cases beat generic podcast hosting language.
- Private podcasting is easier to advertise when tied to courses or communities.
- WordPress workflow may produce better buyer quality even if CPL is not the lowest.

## Recommended Next Actions

1. Create 3 more private podcasting concepts for courses and communities.
2. Keep WordPress Control running as the differentiated angle.
3. Pause Internal Training Audio until there is stronger proof.
4. Rewrite Client Podcast Workflow around agency operational pain.
