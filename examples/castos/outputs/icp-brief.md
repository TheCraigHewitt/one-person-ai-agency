# ICP Brief - Castos

## Executive Summary

Castos should target serious podcast publishers who see audio as a business channel, not a hobby. The strongest Meta ads angle is not "start a podcast." It is "make podcast publishing easier when the podcast already matters to the business."

The highest-fit buyers are brands, agencies, course/community operators, and WordPress-heavy publishers who need control, private audio, or a better workflow.

## Primary Buyer Segments

### B2B SaaS And Service Brands

These teams use podcasts for authority, customer education, founder-led content, or demand generation.

Likely pains:

- inconsistent publishing workflow
- unclear analytics
- difficulty managing distribution
- podcast feels disconnected from the rest of marketing

### Agencies Producing Podcasts For Clients

Agencies need repeatable workflows across multiple shows.

Likely pains:

- too many client accounts
- manual publishing process
- client reporting friction
- need for a stable platform

### Course And Community Businesses

These businesses can use private podcasting for members, lessons, coaching, or internal audio.

Likely pains:

- video-first content is not always convenient
- members want audio on the go
- private feeds are awkward to manage

### WordPress-Heavy Publishers

These buyers want podcast publishing tied closely to their website.

Likely pains:

- WordPress workflow matters
- hosting and publishing are split
- existing tools feel disconnected

## Trigger Events

- launching a branded podcast
- migrating from a beginner host
- adding a private podcast
- agency adds podcasting as a service
- business wants to repurpose content into audio
- team wants audio training or member content

## Pain Points

- podcast hosting feels separate from the marketing stack
- private podcasting requires too many tools
- migration feels risky
- analytics are not actionable
- publishing process is more manual than expected

## Current Alternatives

- beginner podcast hosts
- free platform tools
- legacy podcast hosting
- private RSS workarounds
- video/community platforms used awkwardly for audio

## Buying Objections

- "Will migration be painful?"
- "Is this better than my current host?"
- "Do I need private podcasting?"
- "Can I keep my WordPress workflow?"
- "Will this help me grow?"

## Message Angles

- Podcast hosting for serious publishers
- Private podcasting without duct-taping tools together
- Keep podcast publishing connected to WordPress
- One workflow for public and private audio
- Hosting built for brands, agencies, and communities

## Meta Targeting Implications

Use interest and behavior clusters around podcasting, creator tools, WordPress, course platforms, community platforms, marketing operations, and business content creation.

Creative should qualify the buyer in the hook. Avoid broad beginner language unless testing a top-of-funnel segment.

## Unknowns And Assumptions

- Actual best-converting segment needs campaign data.
- Pricing sensitivity is unknown from these inputs.
- Proof should be pulled from approved Castos assets before final client-facing use.
