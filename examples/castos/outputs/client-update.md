# Client Update - Castos

Here is what I found.

The strongest early angles are not broad "start a podcast" messages. The better direction is to qualify serious publishers with specific use cases:

- podcast hosting for serious publishers
- private podcasting for courses and communities
- WordPress podcast publishing
- agency podcast operations
- migration from a basic host

My recommendation is to test the private podcasting and WordPress angles first. They are more specific, more differentiated, and easier to connect to a real buyer pain.
