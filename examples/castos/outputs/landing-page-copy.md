Most podcast hosting tools are built around the beginner use case: upload an episode, get an RSS feed, move on.

That works until your podcast becomes part of the business.

Then you need better publishing control, private audio options, analytics, WordPress workflow, and a platform that can grow with the show.

## Mechanism
