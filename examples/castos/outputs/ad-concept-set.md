# Ad Concept Set - Castos

## Concept 1: Podcast Hosting For Serious Publishers

Target segment: B2B brands and established creators
Core pain: Podcasting is part of the business, but hosting still feels like a hobby tool.
Hook: Your podcast is a marketing channel. Host it like one.
Visual direction: Clean Castos dashboard with callouts for hosting, analytics, distribution, and private podcasting.
Primary text: If your podcast supports your business, your hosting workflow should not feel duct-taped together. Castos gives serious publishers one place to host, distribute, analyze, and manage public or private audio.
Headline variants:

- Podcast Hosting For Serious Publishers
- Host Your Business Podcast With Castos
- Stop Treating Your Podcast Like A Side Project

Primary text variants:

- Your podcast is part of your marketing engine. Castos helps you host, publish, and manage it with more control.
- Built for publishers who need more than a basic hosting account.
- Public podcast, private podcast, WordPress workflow, analytics. One place to manage it.

CTA: Start Free Trial
Landing page alignment: Hero should lead with "podcast hosting for serious publishers."
Why it might work: Qualifies higher-intent buyers and avoids beginner podcast noise.
Risk / caveat: Needs proof or UI detail to avoid sounding abstract.
