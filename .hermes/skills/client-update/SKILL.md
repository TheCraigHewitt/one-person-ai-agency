# Client Update

Use this skill to draft the client-facing update after the packet is assembled.

## Inputs

- latest client packet
- weekly report
- quality review

## Output

Write `clients/<client>/outputs/client-update.md`.

## Tone

- clear
- practical
- direct
- no overpromising

## Required Shape

1. What we found
2. What I recommend
3. What I need from you
4. Next step
