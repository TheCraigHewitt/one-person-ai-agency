# Client Intake

Use this skill to create or validate a client workspace before research begins.

## Inputs

- `clients/<client>/inputs/client-intake.md`
- `clients/<client>/inputs/offer-notes.md`
- `clients/<client>/inputs/brand-voice.md`
- `clients/<client>/inputs/competitor-list.md`

## Output

Write `clients/<client>/outputs/intake-summary.md`.

## Process

1. Confirm the website, offer, buyer, market, and competitor list exist.
2. Identify missing mission-critical inputs.
3. Summarize the business in plain language.
4. State the assumed Meta ads objective.
5. List what can proceed and what requires Craig's input.

## Rules

- Ask for missing offer or audience details before final strategy work.
- Do not invent proof, pricing, audience claims, or customer data.
