# ICP Research

Use this skill to create a practical ICP brief for Meta ads strategy.

## Inputs

- intake summary
- client website
- offer notes
- customer notes
- optional live web research

## Output

Write `clients/<client>/outputs/icp-brief.md`.

## Required Sections

- Executive summary
- Primary buyer segments
- Trigger events
- Pain points
- Current alternatives
- Buying objections
- Message angles
- Meta targeting implications
- Unknowns and assumptions

## Rules

- Make the ICP specific enough to guide ad creative.
- Separate known facts from assumptions.
- Do not invent customer quotes or market stats.
