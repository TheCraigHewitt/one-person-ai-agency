## Objection Handling

**FAQ block (collapsible):**

**"Is it just water?"**
Yes. That is the joke. The water is real. The branding is the entertainment.

**"$13.59 is a lot for water."**
It is less than two craft beers. Subscribe-and-save brings it under $1.70 per can.

**"Can I cancel?"**
In two clicks. We are not going to make you fight a chatbot.

**"Is this safe to drink at work?"**
The branding is gnarlier than the contents. You can drink it next to a five-year-old. The five-year-old will ask about the skull. You will explain it.

**"What if I just want sparkling?"**
Sparkling variety pack is one toggle away. So is the Armless Palmer iced tea.

**"Do you ship to my apartment / dorm / tour bus?"**
US, Canada, UK, and parts of the EU. Tour buses, yes, if the address holds still.

## Quality review — agency notes

- Copy is in brand voice and clears the "would Smartwater write this" sniff test.
- No health claims. No sobriety/recovery language. Compliance posture safe.
- Subscribe-and-save is the default toggle, matching category leader pattern (AG1, Liquid I.V., Poppi).
- "Cancel anytime" is hit three times intentionally — the data on category cancellation friction is the single biggest objection.
- FAQ language about the brand being gnarlier than the contents addresses parent / workplace objection without flinching.
- Recommend pairing with creator UGC in the proof strip before driving real paid spend to this page.
