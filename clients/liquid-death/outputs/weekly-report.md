## Hypotheses

1. **Bar Hand is winning because the hook collapses two segments at once** (gym + sober-curious). The "drink in your hand" frame works whether the bar is literal or metaphorical. Confirm by running a non-bar-setting variant.

2. **Subscribe attach holding at ~38% suggests the subscription-as-default toggle is doing real work on the landing page.** Worth a UI experiment to push it higher, but the current rate is already category-strong.

3. **The Plastic Body Count failure is about audience, not creative.** Sustainability messaging requires brand familiarity. The creative is fine. The placement is wrong.

4. **ASC+ may quietly outperform manually-targeted campaigns within 2-3 weeks** based on category benchmarks. Plan to migrate 30-40% of budget to ASC+ if early signal holds.
