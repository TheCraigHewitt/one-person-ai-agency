# Competitor List

| Brand | Website | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Waterloo Sparkling Water | waterloosparkling.com | Mainstream sparkling, clean design, occupies the "grown-up LaCroix" slot. Pulls a different shopper but shows up on the same shelf. |
| Spindrift | spindriftfresh.com | Premium sparkling, real-fruit angle, female-skewing aesthetic. Strong on Meta with creator-led UGC. |
| Recess | takearecess.com | CBD/mood-led beverage, pastel aesthetic, sells "vibes." Closest aesthetic anti-pole to Liquid Death — same Gen Z target, opposite mood. |
| Poppi | drinkpoppi.com | Prebiotic soda, biggest cultural mindshare in the functional beverage space right now. Bridge brand from soda quitters into "better-for-you" canned drinks. |
| Olipop | drinkolipop.com | Prebiotic soda, more health-coded than Poppi. Different shelf, overlapping shopper. |
| Celsius | celsius.com | Functional energy, gym-adjacent, sober-curious-adjacent. Real competitor for the gym occasion. |
| Athletic Greens (AG1) | drinkag1.com | Different category but the gold standard for DTC subscription beverage acquisition. Watch their Meta playbook. |
| Liquid I.V. | liquidiv.com | Hydration powders. Targets some of the same gym + festival occasions. Sells "hydration math" in a way Liquid Death refuses to. |
