# Claims Policy

Do not invent:

- performance metrics
- ROAS
- conversion rates
- revenue
- customer quotes
- testimonials
- logos
- case studies
- market stats
- competitor results

Allowed language:

- "Based on the provided inputs..."
- "This suggests..."
- "A reasonable hypothesis is..."
- "This needs validation..."
- "Use this as a draft, not a claim."

Blocked language:

- "Guaranteed"
- "Proven"
- "This will"
- "Customers love"
- "Competitor X is winning because"
